Export as Google Earth KMZ-file

The Time Series Tool extends the BEAM file menu by the entry "Export Time Series as Google Earth KMZ...", which allows to export Time Series as KMZ-files and thus be supported by Google Earth. Screenshot 1 shows an example of a Time Series imported into Google Earth.
