Import RADARSAT-1 Product

Radarsat-1 was Canada’s first Earth Observation mission. The mission operated for 17 years.

Operational: 1995-11-04 to 2013-03-29

Frequency Band: C-band

Acquisition Modes: Stripmap, ScanSAR

Product Types:  

  • SLC Single Look Complex
  • SGF Path Image mulitlooked ground range
  • SGX Path Image Plus mulitlooked ground range
  • SSG Map Image mulitlooked ground range projected
  • SCW ScanSAR Wide
  • SCN ScanSAR Narrow

Polarizations: All modes are Single Pol (HH)

Opening products: Products can be opened by selecting the Volume file. Opening zipped products (*.zip) directly is also supported.
