MERIS/(A)ATSR SMAC Atmospheric Correction

The MERIS/(A)ATSR SMAC Atmospheric Correction implements the Simplified Method for Atmospheric Correction as described by Rahman and Dedieu in:
"SMAC: a simplified method for the atmospheric correction of satellite measurements in the solar spectrum"
H. RAHMAN, G. DEDIEU Int. J. Remote Sensing, 1994, vol.15, no.1, 123-143.

This atmospheric correction method is intended for usage over land.
