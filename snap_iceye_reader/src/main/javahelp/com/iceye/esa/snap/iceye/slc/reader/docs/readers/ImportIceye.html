Iceye is a commercial constellation of smallsat (100kg) SAR satellites. The constellation will have 18 satellites providing revisits of 1 hour.

Operational: 2018-01-12 to present

Frequency Band: X-band

Acquisition Modes: Stripmap, ScanSAR, Spotlight

Product Types:  

  • SLC Single Look Complex
  • GRD Ground Range Detected
  • Ortho terrain corrected
  • PRES terrain flattened

Polarizations: All modes are Single Pol (VV).
